About Homeless Helpers

Our mission, our story, and our commitment to supporting those in need

Our Mission

Homeless Helpers was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide practical resources, support, and dignity to individuals experiencing homelessness. We believe that homelessness is not a reflection of a person's worth or potential, but rather a difficult circumstance that anyone could face.

Our organization works to bridge the gap between available services and the people who need them most, creating accessible pathways to stability and self-sufficiency. We are dedicated to ensuring that every person has access to the basic necessities of life: shelter, food, healthcare, and community connection.

Support

Providing direct assistance and resources to those in need

Advocacy

Speaking up for policy changes to address the roots of homelessness

Our Story

Homeless Helpers began when our founder, Sarah Thompson, experienced a brief period of homelessness after losing her job during an economic downturn. During this challenging time, she discovered the fragmented nature of support services and the difficulties in accessing them without a fixed address or internet access.

After regaining stability, Sarah committed herself to creating a comprehensive resource hub that would make it easier for others to navigate the complex system of services available. She gathered a team of professionals, volunteers, and individuals with lived experience of homelessness to build what would become Homeless Helpers.

Since our founding, we have helped thousands of individuals access shelter, food, healthcare, employment opportunities, and most importantly, hope for a better future. We continue to expand our services based on the direct feedback from the communities we serve.

Our Unique Approach

Digital Access for All

We believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Our website is designed to work even on older devices, with low bandwidth, and through text-only browsers often available at public libraries.

Practical Tools

Our letter generator and documentation services help bridge the gap between bureaucratic requirements and real-world circumstances, making it easier to access services.

Pet-Inclusive Support

We recognize the vital companionship pets provide and work to ensure that having an animal doesn't become a barrier to accessing services and shelter.
