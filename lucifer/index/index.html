Mental Health Support

Access free mental health services designed specifically for those experiencing homelessness:

  • Crisis intervention and emergency mental health support
  • Trauma-informed counselling with specialists
  • Depression, anxiety, and PTSD support groups
  • Medication management and assistance
  • Connection to NHS mental health services

Service Dog Assistance

Our service dog program provides trained companions to support those experiencing homelessness:

  • Assistance with completing service dog certification
  • Connecting with trained emotional support animals
  • Information on your legal rights with service animals
  • Pet-friendly accommodation resources
  • Food and veterinary care support

Housing Rights & Resources

Under British law, you have specific rights when facing homelessness:

  • Local council has a duty to provide assistance
  • Priority need assessment for vulnerable individuals
  • Right to emergency accommodation in certain circumstances
  • Assistance with applying for housing benefits
  • Support challenging unfair housing decisions
Know Your Rights Council Help

How Service Dogs Help During Homelessness

Emotional support and companionship during difficult times
Legal protection under UK law for service animal handlers
Alerts for seizures, anxiety attacks, or other medical emergencies
Help finding dog-friendly emergency shelters and accommodation
Personal security and protection while sleeping rough
