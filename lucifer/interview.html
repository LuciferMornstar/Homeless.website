Founders Interview: Shining a Light on Homeless Help

Lucifer and Laura Morningstar share their journey from homelessness to founding Homeless Help, and how lived experience is changing the system for others.

Interviewer: Your story's a bit different from the usual superhero stuff, right? You actually lived this, yeah?
Lucifer: Yeah, you could say that. We just call it 'getting stuck in.' ...
Laura: Eye-opener? That's putting it mildly, Luce. ...
"You're not just a statistic; you're a human being in crisis, and they treat you like you're a nuisance." - Laura Morningstar
Interviewer: It's like you lose your humanity in their eyes.
Laura: Exactly! And that's what hurts the most. ...

Did You Know?

Homeless Helpers was started in a camper van with two dogs, a laptop, and a lot of stubborn hope. Our founders believe laughter and community are as important as food and shelter. 🐾

System Failures

Lucifer: ...'I don't believe you're homeless.' Just like that. Refused to help us.
Laura: ...We didn't know it at the time, but that was a complete breach of the 56-day rule. ...
Lucifer: ...It's not just the immediate rejection, but the feeling that the very system that's supposed to protect you has turned its back on you. ...
