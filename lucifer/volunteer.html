Volunteer With Us

Make a real difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness by joining our volunteer team.

Volunteer RolesWhy VolunteerVolunteer StoriesGet InvolvedCorporate Volunteering

Volunteer Roles

Buddy System Volunteer

Support someone recently housed, helping them adjust and thrive.

Food Service Volunteer

Help prepare and serve meals at our community kitchens and food banks.

Skills Workshop Leader

Run workshops on budgeting, cooking, IT skills, or creative activities.

Outreach Team Member

Join our street outreach teams to connect with rough sleepers and offer support.

Admin & Office Support

Help with admin tasks, phones, emails, and organizing resources.

Professional Skills Volunteer

Offer your professional skills in legal, healthcare, mental health, or other areas.

Why Volunteer With Us

Develop Skills

Gain valuable experience and develop transferable skills for your personal and professional life.

Join a Community

Become part of a supportive community of passionate volunteers and staff.

Flexible Opportunities

We offer flexible volunteering options to fit your schedule and commitments.

What We Provide

  • Comprehensive training and ongoing support
  • Regular supervision and feedback
  • Reimbursement of reasonable expenses
  • References for future employment
  • A welcoming and inclusive environment
  • Social events and volunteer recognition

Corporate Volunteering

Partner With Us

We offer meaningful corporate volunteering opportunities that benefit your team and make a real difference to those experiencing homelessness.

Team Volunteering Days

  • Community space renovation projects
  • Food preparation and service
  • Organizing donation drives
  • Group outreach activities

Skills-Based Volunteering

  • Professional mentoring
  • IT support and digital inclusion
  • Marketing and communications assistance
  • Financial guidance workshops

Corporate volunteering helps your organization:

  • Strengthen team bonds through shared meaningful experiences
  • Demonstrate corporate social responsibility
  • Develop staff skills and boost morale
  • Create lasting local community connections

To discuss corporate volunteering partnerships, contact our Corporate Engagement Team at volunteer@homeless.website
