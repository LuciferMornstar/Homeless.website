How to Access Food Support

Food Bank Referrals

Most food banks work on a referral system. You can get referred by:

  • Citizen's Advice Bureau
  • GP or health visitor
  • Social worker or support worker
  • Local council welfare team
  • School staff or children's centers

Emergency Food Without Referral

If you need food urgently and can't get a referral:

  • Some independent food banks don't require referrals
  • Community kitchens and soup kitchens usually provide meals without referrals
  • Local religious organizations often provide emergency food assistance
  • For immediate help, call our helpline: 0800 123 4567
