About the verification service

This service allows you to verify the legitimacy of an assistance dog licence issued by MorningstarRescue. This can be useful for:

  • Business owners who need to confirm a service animal's status
  • Housing providers verifying assistance animal documentation
  • Transport services ensuring service animal credentials

Legal Information

Under the Equality Act 2010, service providers, those providing goods and facilities to the public, and those performing public functions must make reasonable adjustments to allow assistance dogs to accompany their owners.

Although this verification service is provided as a courtesy, please note that service animal handlers are not legally required to carry documentation.
