After Housing Support

At Homeless Helpers, we understand that securing housing is just the first step. Many individuals feel isolated and face new challenges after transitioning into stable housing. Our After Housing Support program ensures that no one has to navigate this journey alone.

We provide continued support to help individuals maintain their housing, build community connections, and achieve long-term stability.

Our Services

Regular Check-ins

Dedicated support workers provide regular check-ins to ensure stability and address any challenges.

Mental Health Support

Access to counseling and mental health services tailored to individual needs.

Life Skills Workshops

Workshops on budgeting, cooking, and home maintenance to foster independence.

Community Events

Opportunities to build social connections and a sense of belonging through community events.

Success Stories

Mark's Journey

"After finding housing, I felt more alone than ever. The support network connected me with my buddy, James, who helped me feel part of the community again. The weekly skills workshops gave me confidence in managing my new home, and now I'm even volunteering to help others."

Sarah's Story

"Being part of the network meant that when I faced a crisis with my heating in winter, I wasn't alone. My network connections helped me navigate repairs and provided temporary heating. Without this support, I might have lost my housing again."
