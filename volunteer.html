Volunteer With Us

Make a real difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness by joining our volunteer team.

Volunteer Roles

We offer a variety of volunteer roles to match your skills, interests, and availability. Whether you can spare a few hours a week or want to commit to a regular schedule, we have opportunities for everyone.

Buddy System Volunteer

Provide one-to-one support to someone who has recently moved into housing, helping them adjust to their new environment and tackle everyday challenges.

  • Required time: 2-4 hours per week
  • Training provided: Yes (2 days)
  • DBS check: Required

Food Service Volunteer

Help prepare and serve meals at our community kitchens, food banks, and outreach services across the UK.

  • Required time: Flexible shifts
  • Training provided: Basic food hygiene
  • DBS check: Not required

Skills Workshop Leader

Share your expertise by running workshops on topics such as budgeting, cooking, IT skills, or creative activities.

  • Required time: 2-3 hours per session
  • Training provided: Support materials provided
  • DBS check: May be required

Outreach Team Member

Join our street outreach teams to connect with rough sleepers, offering support, advice, and essentials.

  • Required time: 4-hour shifts (daytime or evening)
  • Training provided: Comprehensive training
  • DBS check: Required

Admin & Office Support

Help with administrative tasks, answering phones, responding to emails, and organizing resources.

  • Required time: Regular weekly slot preferred
  • Training provided: Yes
  • DBS check: May be required

Professional Skills Volunteer

Offer your professional skills in areas such as legal advice, healthcare, mental health support, hairdressing, etc.

  • Required time: By arrangement
  • Requirements: Professional qualifications
  • DBS check: Required

Why Volunteer With Us

Make a Real Impact

Your contribution as a volunteer makes a tangible difference in people's lives. Our volunteers help individuals not just survive, but rebuild their lives and thrive.

Develop Skills

Gain valuable experience and develop transferable skills that enhance your personal and professional life.

Join a Community

Become part of a supportive community of passionate volunteers and staff dedicated to making a difference.

Flexible Opportunities

We offer flexible volunteering options to fit around your existing commitments and schedule.

What We Provide

  • Comprehensive training and ongoing support
  • Regular supervision and feedback
  • Reimbursement of reasonable expenses
  • References for future employment
  • A welcoming and inclusive environment
  • Social events and volunteer recognition

Whether you can give a little time or a lot, your contribution is valued. Join us in building a more compassionate and supportive society for those experiencing homelessness.

Volunteer Stories

Sarah's Story

"I started volunteering as a Buddy after retirement. The connection I've formed with James, who was homeless for 3 years, has been transformative for both of us. Seeing him settle into his home and gain confidence has been incredibly rewarding."

Sarah has been volunteering with us for 2 years. She meets with James weekly to help with practical tasks and provide companionship.

Raj's Story

"As a chef, I wanted to use my skills to give back. I now lead cooking workshops teaching basic, affordable recipes. The participants are so engaged and grateful - it's the highlight of my week!"

Raj volunteers in our life skills program, teaching cooking classes twice a month to individuals who have recently moved into housing.

Team Horizon's Story

"Our corporate volunteering day renovating a community space was eye-opening. We left with a stronger team bond and a real sense that we'd contributed to something important."

A team from Horizon Technologies spent a day refurbishing a community room used for group activities and support meetings.
