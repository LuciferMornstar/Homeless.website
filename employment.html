Employment Resources

Find job search help, training, and support to build a sustainable career

How We Help With Employment

CV & Application Support

Get help creating or updating your CV, writing cover letters, and completing job applications. In-person and remote support available.

Interview Preparation

Practice interviews, receive feedback, and gain confidence. We can also provide interview clothing through partner organizations.

Employer Partnerships

Connect with inclusive employers offering flexible work and supportive environments for people overcoming homelessness.

Training & Education

Vocational Training

  • Construction & trades (CSCS card)
  • Food hygiene & hospitality
  • Care work & healthcare support
  • IT skills & digital literacy
  • Retail & customer service

Courses run throughout the year at various locations or online.

Soft Skills Workshops

  • Communication & teamwork
  • Time management
  • Work readiness
  • Confidence building
  • Financial literacy

Weekly workshops at community centers or online.

Educational Support

  • College application assistance
  • Access to course materials and laptops
  • Information on grants and financial support
  • Mentoring throughout your educational journey
Education Resources

Overcoming Employment Barriers

Address & Documentation

Use our address service for job applications and get help obtaining ID, birth certificates, and NI numbers.

Transportation

Get help with bus passes, bike loans, and travel planning for interviews and the first weeks of work.

Digital Access

Use our computer centers for job searches or borrow devices for remote applications and interviews.

Need More Help?

Our advisors are here to support you with every step of your employment journey. If you need one-to-one help, contact us today.
