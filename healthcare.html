Healthcare Resources

Access healthcare resources for homeless individuals across the UK.

Mental Health Support

Information on counselling, crisis hotlines, and community mental health services.

Physical Health Services

Details on free clinics, GP registrations, and mobile health units.

Medication Assistance

Guidance on obtaining prescriptions, subsidies and support for long-term treatments.

GP Registration

GP practices cannot refuse to register you because you don't have a fixed address or identification. Use a day centre or hostel address (with permission), register as having "no fixed abode," or contact PALS if registration is refused.

Medical Emergencies

In case of emergencies, call 999 or go to your nearest A&E. For non-emergency advice, dial 111, visit a walk-in centre, or speak to a pharmacist.

Substance Use & Recovery Services

Find drop-in harm reduction services, needle exchanges, community drug and alcohol teams, detox and rehabilitation programmes. Self-refer directly or call the Frank helpline on 0300 123 6600 for confidential support.

Additional Support

Our team can accompany you to appointments, explain medical information, advocate with providers, and help set up medication reminders and management systems.
